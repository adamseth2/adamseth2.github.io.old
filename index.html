Covid-19 Precautions

When we confirm your appointment, we will ask you some pre-screening questions.

These will be repeated when you arrive for your appointment.

Please call us from your car when you arrive, do not enter the office!
(901) 815-8391

We will ask the prescreening questions again and let you know when you should enter the office. You will need to wear a mask or face covering when you enter the office.

You will have your temperature taken, please do not leave the waiting room until this is done. Only patients receiving treatment should enter the office unless the patient requires a care giver or a parent. Friends and family will need to stay in the car.

Please limit the items you bring into the office as we will ask you to leave them on your lap during your cleaning.

Many of these steps are taken to increase social distancing to ensure the health of our patients and staff.

Please give us a call to set up your appointment!
